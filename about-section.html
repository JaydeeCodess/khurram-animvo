Our Story

ABOUT US

In a digital world where people scroll faster than they blink, Motion Graphix stands as the bridge between ideas and impact.

We see it every day — brilliant products, powerful services, and groundbreaking solutions struggling to connect with the people they're built for. Not because they lack value, but because they aren't explained clearly enough. That's where we step in.

We take complex ideas and turn them into simple, engaging stories — stories that don't just inform, but inspire action. Whether you're launching something new, simplifying something technical, or boosting your brand's voice, we craft animated videos that captivate, clarify, and convert.

From SaaS explainers to product demos and motion graphics, we bring your message to life in a way your audience gets — and remembers. We are Motion Graphix. We help businesses move forward through the power of animated storytelling.

Founder Story

Meet Zahid, the passionate founder of Motion Graphix Pvt. Ltd., where creativity brings ideas to life. With a strong love for storytelling and exceptional skills as a 2D animator and storyboard designer, Zahid is the visionary behind the studio's inspiring work.

His journey in animation began with a spark of inspiration, turning into a dedicated pursuit of excellence. This passion led to the creation of Motion Graphics — a studio known for its captivating 2D animations and engaging stories.

Zahid's ability to create meaningful narratives and bring characters to life is at the heart of the studio's success. His talent and dedication continue to inspire and deliver animations that connect with audiences worldwide.
